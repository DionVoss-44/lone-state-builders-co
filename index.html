    05 · Process

    How a bid becomes a finished opening.

    1. 01

      Plans in

      You upload drawings & specs above, or email them directly. ZIPs and DWG sets are fine.

      Same day ack
    2. 02

      Takeoff & hardware schedule

      We run the door schedule and build hardware sets to spec — cross-checking fire ratings, ADA, and electrified trim.

      Days 1–3
    3. 03

      Bid delivered

      Line-item material bid plus install labor — a document your GC or owner can drop into their spreadsheet.

      By day 5
    4. 04

      Submittals & order

      Approved submittals, shop drawings, and release to Texas-based manufacturers. Lower freight, faster delivery.

      Weeks 1–4
    5. 05

      Install

      Houston or Dallas crew on site. Kitted by opening, coordinated with your super, clean punch list.

      On schedule
    6. 06

      Close-out

      As-built door schedules, keying, warranties, and O&M docs handed over for the project file.

      Final walk
